Our Services

Elevate Your Event with World-Class Chamber Music

Why Choose Cuarteto Metanoia?

With years of experience performing at prestigious venues and events, we bring elegance, professionalism, and exceptional artistry to every occasion. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large celebration, our versatile repertoire and refined sound create unforgettable moments.

Private Concerts

Intimate performances for your exclusive events, featuring a curated program tailored to your preferences.

Includes:

  • Custom repertoire selection
  • Pre-concert consultation
  • Professional sound setup
  • Duration: 45-90 minutes
  • Perfect for house concerts and private gatherings

Ideal for: Art collectors, music enthusiasts, intimate celebrations

Corporate Events

Add sophistication to your corporate functions with live classical music that impresses clients and energizes teams.

Includes:

  • Professional presentation
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Background or featured performance
  • Suitable for conferences and galas
  • Enhances brand image

Ideal for: Product launches, annual dinners, networking events

Weddings & Celebrations

Create magical moments on your special day with elegant live music for ceremonies and receptions.

Includes:

  • Ceremony music (processional, recessional)

  • Popular and classical repertoire

Recording & Production

Professional studio recordings for albums, soundtracks, and commercial projects with exceptional sound quality.

Includes:

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for weddings and major events. However, we can often accommodate shorter timelines depending on our schedule.

What is your performance area?

We primarily serve Bogotá and surrounding regions, but we're happy to travel for events throughout Colombia and internationally.

Can you play non-classical music?

Absolutely! While our specialty is classical repertoire, we perform arrangements of popular music, film scores, jazz standards, and traditional Colombian music.

What equipment do you provide?

We bring all necessary music stands and can provide amplification for larger venues. For outdoor events, we recommend you provide weather protection.

Do you offer virtual performances?

Yes, we can provide high-quality live-streamed or pre-recorded performances for virtual events.
